Synopsis

Ruby is at rock-bottom on a bathroom floor, until she catches Roxanne's eye in the mirror. Instantly attracted to her confidence, Roxanne is everything Ruby wishes she could be.

As their relationship develops Ruby changes, becoming more and more brave, but this turns to recklessness and pushing everyone else away. She suddenly realises that she's lost herself along the way, this scares her and she tells Roxanne to leave, but can she find herself again?
